Introduction to German Noun Genders (Einführung in die deutschen Substantivgeschlechter)

[မြန်မာဘာသာဖြင့် ရှင်းလင်းချက်]

In German, every noun has a grammatical gender. It can be masculine, feminine, or neuter. You can tell the gender by the article used with the noun ("the").

  • Masculine: der
  • Feminine: die
  • Neuter: das

It's important to learn the gender with each noun, as it affects the grammar of the sentence. The gender of a noun is not always logical.
