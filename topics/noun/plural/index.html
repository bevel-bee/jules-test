Plural Nouns

Forming plurals in German is more complex than in English. There are several different endings, and you need to learn the plural form for each noun.

The good news is that in the plural, the definite article ("the") is always die for all genders.

Common Plural Endings

Plural EndingSingularPluralBurmese (Placeholder)
-e der Tisch die Tische the tables
-er das Buch die Bücher the books
-n / -en die Lampe die Lampen the lamps
-s (often for foreign words) das Auto die Autos the cars
No ending (sometimes with an umlaut change) der Apfel die Äpfel the apples
